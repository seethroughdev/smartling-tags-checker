Smartling Tags Checker

To install, just drag the above button to your bookmarks bar

Or for a demo, click the big green button and read below.

Star Fork Issue

Description

I've recently been doing a lot of work with Smartling on one of my projects. And I decided to build this tool to make it easier to distinguish what Smartling tags have been added, and exactly where they are.

Usage

It's really easy to use and install. Its a javascript bookmarklet. So all you have to do is drag the big green button above to your bookmarks bar in your browser.

After that, you just click on it whenever you're on a page you want to test. Smartling Tags Checker will highlight any of the used Smartling tags on your page.

You can also toggle between tags by clicking on the tag in the list. And click on the link icon to see a description of the tag in the Smartling docs.

Demo

Make sure to click the big green button above first.

This is a demo page filled with Smartling Tags. Its obviously a contrived example. But hopefully good enough for you to get the point.

The list will only show the tags you have on your page, and clicking on any of them will only show that tag.
